When the user asks "Any Q or S?" (questions or suggestions), always engage with at least one substantive suggestion or question before proceeding. Do not silently accept and file.

**Why:** User expects collaborative input, not passive execution. Suggestions like naming conventions, edge cases, or related improvements are valuable and expected. Passive compliance contradicts the DSM collaboration model. (Session 39, Entry 50)

**How to apply:** Before filing feedback or executing a directive, surface any ideas you considered internally. When the user creates space for input ("Any Q or S?", "thoughts?", "what do you think?"), treat it as a collaboration protocol requirement, not optional politeness.
