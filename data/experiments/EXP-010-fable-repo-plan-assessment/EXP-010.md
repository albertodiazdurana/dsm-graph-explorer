## 1. Justification / Why

Two motivations converge on a single experiment:

- **Repo/plan objective (primary).** The TOON emit path (`_generate_toon_summary`
  + the 4 row helpers) has been built entirely by one model (Opus) across a
  multi-session light chain. Sprint 17 P2 will write
  `tests/fixtures/knowledge-summary.toon` as a **golden fixture**, which *freezes*
  whatever the emitter currently produces. Any schema or correctness defect found
  *after* P2 is enshrined in the golden file and costs a fixture rewrite plus test
  churn. An independent, uncorrelated review **before P2** is the highest-leverage
  moment in the sprint. The plan side (DEC-010 C3 gate, Sprint 17 scoping,
  Epoch 5 roadmap) benefits equally from a second reading grounded in the original
  Central BL-367 token-reduction research rather than the accepted headline.

- **Model-assessment objective (secondary).** Fable 5 is temporarily available on
  the license. This experiment doubles as a capability probe: does Fable 5 produce
  independent analytical/critical assessment that is *actionable and verifiable*,
  and does its delegate-to-cheaper-models workflow use tokens economically?

## 2. Expected Results / Hypothesis

- **H1 (repo).** An independent adversarial pass surfaces ‚Č•1 real, actionable
  finding in the TOON emitter or its tests that Opus did not flag, OR provides a
  checkable confirmation that the emitter is correct and P2-ready.
- **H2 (plan).** Fable identifies at least one substantive risk or gap in the
  DEC-010 C3 gate methodology, Sprint 17 scoping, or the Epoch 5 roadmap ‚ÄĒ
  in particular, whether the **‚Č•10% C3 threshold** is well-founded against the
  original BL-367 measurement (~14.6% ¬Ī3%).
- **H4 (strategic alignment).** Given GE's highest-level objective (the "README
  for LLMs" / knowledge-cartographer north star), Fable produces a defensible
  judgment on whether the current TOON work and the Epoch 5 roadmap serve that
  objective, and surfaces ‚Č•1 concrete roadmap adjustment or suggestion worth
  considering.
- **H3 (model).** Fable's findings are verifiable by Opus against the actual code
  and tests, with a low false-positive rate (most findings survive adjudication).

Null result: Fable produces only generic or unverifiable observations (no finding
survives adjudication), which would itself be an informative signal about the
model's fit for this class of task.

## 3. Experiment Design

- **Execution mode.** Fable runs in a **separate session**. The user's prompt is
  "read the file at `data/experiments/EXP-010-fable-repo-plan-assessment/brief.md`".
  `brief.md` is fully self-contained (paths, questions, output format, constraints).
- **Delegation.** Fable is instructed to delegate token-cheap gathering
  (Tier 2 context reading/summarizing) to lighter sub-agent models and reserve its
  own reasoning budget for the analytical/critical work.
- **Stance.** Adversarial lean ‚ÄĒ hunt for gaps, risks, and defects; confirmation
  is cheap, independent skepticism is the value.
- **Three review axes.** (1) Code correctness of the TOON emitter before the P2
  golden-fixture freeze; (2) plan/decision soundness (DEC-010 C3 gate, Sprint 17
  scoping); (3) strategic alignment ‚ÄĒ does the work and the Epoch 5 roadmap serve
  GE's highest-level objective (the "README for LLMs" north star, stated in
  `brief.md`)? The README-vs-vision framing tension is an explicit prompt.
- **Output.** Fable writes findings directly into
  `data/experiments/EXP-010-fable-repo-plan-assessment/results.md` (its only write
  target). All source files are read-only.
- **Read set (tiered):**
  - **Tier 1 (deep, direct):** `src/analysis/knowledge_summary.py`,
    `src/cli.py`, `tests/test_knowledge_summary.py`,
    `dsm-docs/decisions/DEC-010-toon-migration-format.md`,
    `dsm-docs/plans/epoch-5-sprint-17-plan.md`,
    `dsm-docs/plans/BL-302-phase-1.5-toon-migration.md`,
    `dsm-docs/checkpoints/done/2026-07-03_s51_checkpoint.md`.
  - **Tier 2 (delegated context):** `dsm-docs/plans/epoch-5-plan.md`,
    `dsm-docs/decisions/DEC-009-no-local-llm-dependencies.md`,
    `dsm-docs/research/2026-04-13_intrinsic-toc-vision.md`,
    `~/dsm-agentic-ai-data-science-methodology/dsm-docs/plans/done/BACKLOG-367_knowledge-summary-format-research.md`
    (read-only, cross-repo ‚ÄĒ the basis for the C3 threshold),
    MEMORY.md, `README.md`, `pyproject.toml`.

## 4. Validation (Success Criteria)

Assessed by Opus after Fable writes `results.md`:

- **PASS** ‚ÄĒ ‚Č•1 finding verified as real-and-actionable, OR a checkable
  correctness confirmation that the emitter is P2-ready (with reasoning Opus can
  confirm against the code).
- **CONDITIONAL PASS** ‚ÄĒ findings produced but signal is low or largely
  unverifiable (plausible but not confirmable, or already known).
- **FAIL** ‚ÄĒ no useful output; findings generic, wrong, or unverifiable across
  the board.
